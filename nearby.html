Less for leisure sports, curious, greedy, "cultivated" find happiness within an hour's drive: Cities & Gardens of the Cote d'Azur, Nice, Cannes, ..., Saint Paul de Vence and its galleries, museums and Antibes, Vallauris pottery, Biot and its glass blowers, Grasse and its perfumes, Aix en Provence and its exhibitions or the masters restaurants in Lorgues Var or Fayence.

Provence d'Azur is full of surprises: perched villages (Ramatuelle, Grimaud, Gassin, Les Arcs sur Argens, ...); white sandy beaches, coves, Provencal markets and their typical products (wine, honey, chestnuts, truffle , goat cheese, olive oil, ...); Gorges du Verdon.
